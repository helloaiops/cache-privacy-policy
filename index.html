Privacy Policy

Cache: Expense & Money Tracker

Developed by Pritesh Koshiya  ·  Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cache: Expense & Money Tracker ("Cache" or "the App"), developed by Pritesh Koshiya, collects, uses, and protects your information.

Summary: Your data stays on your device. Sign-in is optional. We do not sell your data. Notification access is optional and used only to help detect transactions on your phone.

1. Information We Collect

Expense and category data
When you add transactions or categories, they are stored locally on your device using SQLite. If you sign in with Google, this data may be synced to Firebase Firestore under your account.

Google account information
If you choose to sign in, we collect your Google account name and email address via Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication. Sign-in is optional — the App works fully without it.

Banking and payment notifications (Android)
If you enable Notification Access, the App can read the text shown in your notification shade from supported apps (for example, bank or payment alerts). This may include SMS app notifications only if your SMS app displays message content as a notification. Cache does not read your SMS inbox or message history and does not request SMS permissions. Notification content is processed on-device to detect transactions. Raw notification text is not sold or shared with third parties. If you are signed in, parsed fields (such as amount, bank name, and merchant) may be stored in your Firebase account as part of your transaction data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Transaction and category data is used solely to provide expense tracking, budgets, and analytics in the App.
  • Your Google name and email are used to identify your account and associate synced data in Firebase.
  • Notification content is used only to suggest or create transaction entries on your device.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, your expense and category data is additionally stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud), scoped to your user account (Firebase UID) and not accessible to other users.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing. Your data is stored on your device and, if you sign in, in your Firebase account. We do not use advertising SDKs. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy and provide clear notice in the App.

5. Your Data Controls

  • Export: Export your data from Settings in the App (CSV or JSON, depending on version).
  • Sign out: Signing out stops further cloud sync. Local data remains on your device until you delete it.
  • Revoke notification access: Android Settings → Special app access → Notification access → Cache (or turn off in App Settings).
  • Delete local data: Use Delete data / clear options in App Settings.
  • Delete cloud data: Request account and cloud data deletion by emailing us at the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
