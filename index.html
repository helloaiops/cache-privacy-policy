Privacy Policy

A6: Expense Manager & Wallet  ·  Last updated: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how A6: Expense Manager & Wallet ("the App"), developed by Pritesh Koshiya, handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Expense and category data
When you add expenses or categories, they are stored locally on your device using SQLite. If you sign in with Google, this data is also synced to Firebase Firestore under your account.

Google account information
If you choose to sign in, we collect your Google account name and email address via Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication. Sign-in is optional — the App works fully without it.

Banking notification content
The App optionally reads Android notifications from banking apps to auto-detect and create expense entries. This is processed entirely on-device. The raw notification text is never transmitted to any server. Only the parsed fields (amount, bank name, account) are synced to Firebase if you are signed in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Expense and category data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App.
  • Your Google name and email are used to identify your account and associate your synced data in Firebase.
  • Notification content is used only to automatically create expense entries on your device.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, your expense and category data is additionally stored in Firebase Firestore, which is a Google Cloud service. Data is scoped to your user account (identified by your Firebase UID) and is not accessible to other users.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only stored on your device and in your Firebase account. No analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs are used in this App.

5. Your Data Controls

  • Export: You can export all your expense data as a JSON file from Settings → Export History.
  • Sign out: Signing out from Settings stops further cloud sync. Your local data remains on your device.
  • Revoke notification access: Android Settings → Special App Access → Notification Access → A6.
  • Delete cloud data: To request deletion of your data from Firebase, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
