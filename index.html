Privacy Policy

A6: Expense Manager & Wallet  ·  Last updated: May 21, 2026

👋 Overview

A6: Expense Manager & Wallet ("the App") is a personal finance application developed by Pritesh Koshiya. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

The App is designed to work offline. Your expense data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync with your Google account.

💾 How Data is Stored

All expense data, categories, and settings are stored locally on your device using SQLite. No expense data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

If you sign in with Google and enable sync, your data is stored in your personal Google Drive account. Only you have access to it — we do not have access to your Google Drive files.

🔔 Notification Listener Permission

The App optionally requests the Notification Listener permission on Android. This allows the App to read incoming notifications from banking apps to automatically detect and create expense entries.

  • This permission is entirely optional — the App works fully without it.
  • Notification content is processed only on-device and is never sent to any server.
  • Only notifications that match known bank transaction patterns are used.
  • You can revoke this permission at any time from Android Settings → Special App Access → Notification Access.

🔗 Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party service:

  • Google Sign-In — Used for optional account authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

The App does not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, crash reporting services, or tracking tools.

🔒 Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, encryption). When syncing via Google, data is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to your personal Google Drive.

We do not operate any servers that store your personal or financial information.

👶 Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

⚙️ Your Rights & Controls

  • View your data: All your data is visible within the App.
  • Delete your data: Go to Settings → Delete All Data to permanently remove all local records.
  • Revoke Google access: Sign out from Settings, or revoke access from your Google account's security page.
  • Revoke notification access: Android Settings → Apps → A6 → Permissions.
